Telecom Service Solution

Aiolus leverages our partnership with Mobile Network Operators and Original Equipment Manufacturers to provide network engineering services to ensure superior voice, data and content services/infra solutions to the end-user while maximizing ROI for carriers. The primary goal is to minimize the total cost of network deployment and management, while delivering and maintaining a superior wireless network. To efficiently maintain existing legacy networks and migrate or deploy new 4G networks infra, Aiolus continues to focus on innovations in build out, and maintenance managed services/infra solutions to drive superior end-user experience and achieve business efficiencies.

Aiolus leverages our expertise in IT to design and develop solutions to enable virtual teams for remote managed services and performance analytics solutions for network infra optimization.

Aiolus continues to expand our expertise in multi-vendor networks helping clients in deploying and managing networks. From network design to flexible managed services operations and maintenance, Aiolus and its staff of highly trained network engineers are ready to complete your project on time, on budget, and exceed your expectations.
