SATCOM (Satellite Communication)

What Is Satcom ?

Satcom systems was founded on a simple premise that Internet over satellite requires a discipline and focus that Broadcast or Telecom-Oriented Providers simply cannot deliver. The platforms and protocols are different, the customers more demanding. Accordingly, we built VSAT Systems from the ground up around four core principles :

VSAT Services offered by Aiolus Tech is a satellite-based communications service capable of supporting Internet, data, video, LAN, voice/fax communications, and provides powerful, dependable private and public network communications solutions. Increasingly popular, VSATs are a single, flexible communications platform that can be installed quickly and cost effectively to provide connectivity solutions for government and enterprise requirements. The benefits of VSAT technology are being realized in many sectors, both private and public - from banks to administrations, schools, hospitals, and rural telecommunications, VSATs are being seized upon to elevate economic, educational, and health standards.

Aiolus Technologies takes pride in the research and development of new technologies and services, further opening the satellite market to a broad range of users and providing partners with fast and reliable services. Aiolus Technologies provides satellite connectivity across Asia.
